Welcome to Duck!

No Builders, No Themes, Pure Code!

Greetings Fellow Terran or other Creature of the universe. Wherever you come from, I thank you dearly for visiting my website in which I 've put so much passion, energy and love, as I do with every project I undertake. It is intended as a place to showcase my work, and to host my blog in which I often share my thoughts about things that were, things that are, and some things that have not yet come to pass.
For this website I took inspiration from old games like Wing Commander and Unreal Tournament. Furthermore, my other intentions for this site is for the visitor to perceive it as an "experience" of some sort of artform, that is meant to bestow serenity to the restless mind.
You can take a look through my portfolio below, browse through my blog or contact me via the "contact page".
